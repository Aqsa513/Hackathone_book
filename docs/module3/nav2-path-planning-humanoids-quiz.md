## Answer Key

1. B) Bipedal locomotion and balance constraints
2. C) Navigation Stack
3. B) Behavior trees
4. A, B, C, D (All of the above)
5. B) To account for stepability and balance constraints
6. B) Pluggable architecture
7. B) Balance preservation and step-based control
8. C) Both Isaac Sim and Isaac ROS
9. B) Balance recovery and step failure recovery
10. D) All of the above
